What are semantic elements?

Semantic elements describe the meaning of the element to both the browser and developer.

Why symantic elements:

  • They help us avoid "divitis". Instead of using divs and spans we can use elements that clearly desribe their purpose
  • Lots of cool elements like 'pre', 'figure', 'mark', and 'canvas'
